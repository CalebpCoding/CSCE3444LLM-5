About Us

We opened our first mango juice sauna in 2020 with a vision to bring the healing properties of mangoes to the wellness community. Founders Jane and John both experienced the benefits of mango saunas during travels in Southeast Asia and were inspired to share this experience with others.

Our saunas use only the freshest, locally-sourced mangoes to make our juice. We carefully monitor temperatures and session times to ensure a safe yet effective experience. Hygiene and cleanliness are our top priorities to maintain a relaxing environment for all.
